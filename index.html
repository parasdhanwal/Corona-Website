CORONAVIRUS !


Introduction

The first case of coronavirus was detected in China on November 19,2019 . It has caused more than 773K deaths all over the world. The symptoms may be detected from 7 to 14 days after being infected by the virus. Each country in the world is taking preventive measures to protect their public from the novel coronavirus. More rarely, the disease can be serious and even fatal. Older people, and people with other medical conditions (such as asthma, diabetes, or heart disease), may be more vulnerable to becoming severely ill.

The symptoms of novel coronavirus are :
  1. Cough
  2. Fever
  3. Tiredness
  4. Difficulty in breathing
  5. Lost of sense of smell and taste
